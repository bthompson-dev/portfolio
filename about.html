About me

I’ve always been interested in the web and how it works. So when I first needed a personal website, I decided to try and put it together myself. How hard could it be…?

A few months later, after some YouTube tutorials and heavy googling… I had a working website online.

The satisfaction of finally figuring it out started me on a journey to learn more and push myself further to see what I could create.

I’ve always enjoyed challenging myself. That’s one of the things I like about web development – there’s always more to learn. New technologies and approaches, different ways of doing things you haven’t considered.

I’ve worked in a diverse range of roles, from private tutor to professional actor, but the main thing that’s motivated me is finding that challenge in whatever I’m doing.

As a full-stack developer, I’m interested in creating user-friendly, innovative web applications that solve real problems. I enjoy using React, JavaScript, and NodeJS – but am always interested to try out new languages and tech.

When not coding my latest side-project, I can be found listening to new music, playing tennis, competing in online chess or walking my dog Rosa.
